BlueMail Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24th of May, 2026

BlueMail values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Introduction
We, BlueMail, are a company based in Germany. As such, all personal data we handle is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws. By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
We limit the collection of your data to the minimum required to operate our services effectively:
Email Address: We collect your email address to provide access to your mailbox.
App-Specific Password: For iCloud Mail accounts, we collect an Apple app-specific password to enable secure communication with the iCloud Mail service.

We do not collect or store your emails, contacts, or any other personal content from your email account. All other email data remains managed and stored by your email provider (e.g., Apple for iCloud Mail).

3. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:
To enable authentication and secure access to your email account.
To maintain proper functionality of the BlueMail application.
To support customer requests and troubleshoot issues, where applicable.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security
Local Storage: The only data stored by BlueMail is saved locally on your device and is fully encrypted.
Keychain Protection: Your email and app-specific password are stored in Apple’s Keychain, protected by the Secure Enclave technology.
No Cloud Storage by Us: We do not store any of your personal data on our servers. All your emails, attachments, and account data remain with your email provider.

5. Data Protection
We implement strict technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:
End-to-end encryption of sensitive credentials.
Compliance with GDPR requirements.
Utilisation of native platform security features like Apple’s Secure Enclave.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR
As a user based in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
Correction: You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
Deletion: You may request deletion of your data stored in the application.
Objection and Restriction: You may object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: fly@blueairplane.net

7. Data Retention
Because no personal data is stored on our servers, we retain only the data on your device for as long as you use the application. Removing the app from your device deletes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. The latest version will always be available within the app or on our website.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us at:
